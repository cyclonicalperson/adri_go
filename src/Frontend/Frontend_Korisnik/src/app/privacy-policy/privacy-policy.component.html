Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Digital Tourist Map of Montenegro ("AdriGo") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile data: Language preference, interests, bio, and profile picture (optional).
  • Location data: With your permission, we use your GPS coordinates to show nearby attractions and calculate distances.
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, locations saved, reviews submitted, and other in-app interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the AdriGo experience.
  • To show you nearby locations based on your position.
  • To enable features such as saving locations, calendar planning, and reviews.
  • To improve the app and understand how it is used.
  • To send important service updates (not marketing, unless you opt in).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregate statistics with tourism organisations to help improve services in Montenegro. Your personal information is never shared without your explicit consent.

5. Location Data

Location access is optional. If you grant permission, your position is used only within the app session to calculate distances and suggest nearby places. It is not stored on our servers beyond the current session.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed password storage, to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time through the Account section. You may also request complete data deletion by contacting us.

8. Cookies & Analytics

AdriGo uses minimal analytics to understand usage patterns. No advertising cookies are used. You can opt out of anonymous analytics in the Settings menu.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@adrigo.me.
